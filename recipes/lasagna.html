Lasagna

Description:

this is lasagna. It is good. You make it very carefully. Yum. Yum......Yum

Ingredients

  • Noodles
  • Sauce
  • Tomatos
  • Ground Beef
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. preheat oven to 350 degrees
  2. Cook the ground beef until browned, set aside when done.
  3. While beef is cooking, lay our your pan
  4. Chop tomatoes
  5. Oil pan, then line the bottom with a layer of noodles
  6. Brush that layer with a generous amount of tomato sauce and chopped tomatos
  7. once ground beef has finished browning, put your desired amount on the bottom layer of noodle
  8. cover with another layer of noodles and repeat the layering steps
  9. once complete, cover top layer with extra cheese
  10. cover the pan with tin foil
  11. bake for 45 min, before checking to see when its done.
  12. If you would like a crispy top layer, take foil cover off and bake until golden brown...or desired color is reached
